16 US Companies With Offices in Tokyo

Tokyo’s a booming business hub is bolstered by these top U.S. companies.

Written by Ana Gore
Photo: Shutterstock
UPDATED BY
Rose Velazquez | Oct 17, 2025

Tokyo is home to ramen vending machines, the Shinkansen high-speed railway and over 9 million people who build its workforce. The metropolitan area is also home to over 40,000 Americans, but that only begins to encompass the city’s relationship with the United States. We’ve rounded up a list of some of the top U.S. companies that have offices in Tokyo and keep the city’s busy business sector humming. 

US Companies with Tokyo Offices

  • IBM
  • Google
  • Apple 
  • Microsoft
  • Dell Technologies
