18 Tech Companies in Tokyo to Know

These companies offer services ranging from fertility support and luggage storage to game development.

Written by Mia Goulart
Photo: Shutterstock
UPDATED BY
Rose Velazquez | Nov 19, 2025

Known as the “Vending Machine Capital of the World,” Tokyo is famous for its vast number of vending machines — one for every 40 people, adding up to millions in total. 

Much like the variety of snacks these machines offer, the city’s tech scene is just as diverse, with companies working in areas ranging from software to healthcare.

For job seekers hungry to experience the city’s tech landscape, check out the following companies.
